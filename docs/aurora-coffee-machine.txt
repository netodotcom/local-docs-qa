Aurora X200 Coffee Machine — Owner's Guide

Overview

The Aurora X200 is a countertop espresso machine with a 1.8 liter removable
water tank, a 15-bar pump, and an integrated conical burr grinder. It supports
both whole beans and pre-ground coffee.

Daily use

Fill the water tank up to the MAX line and press the power button. The machine
takes about 45 seconds to reach brewing temperature. The ready light turns
solid green when it is safe to brew. For a single espresso press the small cup
button once; for a double espresso press it twice within one second.

Descaling

Descale the Aurora X200 every three months, or every 60 days if your water is
hard. Use only citric-acid based descaler. Fill the tank with one liter of
water and one sachet of descaler, place a large container under the spout, then
hold the steam button for five seconds until the descale light blinks orange.
The full cycle takes about 25 minutes and rinses automatically at the end.

Troubleshooting

If the ready light blinks red, the water tank is empty or seated incorrectly.
If coffee flows too slowly, the grind setting is too fine: turn the grinder
dial one step coarser. Grounds in the cup usually mean the filter basket needs
replacement; baskets are rated for roughly 400 brews. The machine has a
two-year limited warranty covering the pump and heating element.
