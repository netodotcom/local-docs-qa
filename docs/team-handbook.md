# Team Handbook (excerpt)

## Working hours and time off

Core collaboration hours are 10:00 to 15:00 in the Sao Paulo timezone. Outside
of those hours, everyone is free to arrange their own schedule. Full-time
employees accrue 30 days of paid vacation per year, and unused days can be
carried over until March 31 of the following year.

## Expenses

Any work-related expense under R$ 200 can be reimbursed without prior
approval; just attach the receipt in the expenses tool within 30 days. Expenses
above R$ 200 need written approval from your team lead before purchase.
Equipment (laptops, monitors, chairs) is always purchased by the company
directly, never reimbursed.

## Incident response

When a production incident is declared, the on-call engineer becomes the
incident commander by default. The first action is always to open a dedicated
incident channel named #inc-YYYYMMDD-short-description. Status updates go out
every 30 minutes until the incident is mitigated. A blameless post-mortem is
due within five working days of resolution.
